01
The Mission

Our goal is to provide a platform for individuals and organizations to come together and share ideas, resources, and expertise in a transparent and decentralized manner. By leveraging the power of Web3 technologies such as blockchain, we aim to create a self-sustaining ecosystem that empowers individuals to drive innovation and make a positive impact on the world.

We believe that technology has the potential to transform the world in profound ways, but we also recognize the need to use it responsibly and ethically. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of ethical conduct and ensuring that our platform is used for the greater good of humanity.

Ultimately, our vision is to build a global community of scholars who are united in their commitment to collaboration, innovation, sustainability, and ethical use of technology. Through our collective efforts, we aim to create a brighter future for all.

02
The Community


The core foundation of DAO+ is established on a vibrant and varied assembly of people, all of whom possess a shared enthusiasm for blockchain technology and decentralized governance. This community's members enjoy exclusive access to events, workshops, and discussion platforms, enabling them to network with those who share their interests and cultivate meaningful connections within the ecosystem.

03
The Fund

The financial backbone of DAO+ aims to support innovative projects and ventures within the blockchain and broader technology ecosystems. The DAO+ fund is structured as a venture capital (VC) and private equity (PE) fund, which raises money from limited partners (LPs) who share a vision for supporting innovative and impactful projects across different industries and technologies, operating under decentralized management through the community.
04
The Accelerator

DAO+ Accelerator is dedicated to supporting and accelerating innovative blockchain-based projects. As a vital part of our ecosystem, the accelerator works closely with the DAO+ Fund, which screens and selects the most promising projects from the proposal pool.
